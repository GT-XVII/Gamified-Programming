const TARGET_RETAINED_TRACES = 32

The loose upper bound on the number of events that should be retained in a trace. This may be exceeded by up to a factor of 2. Arbitrarily chosen as a number that should be large enough to contain the recent relevant information, but small enough to not use excessive memory.
