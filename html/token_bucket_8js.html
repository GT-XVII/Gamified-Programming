A hierarchical token bucket for rate limiting. See http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Token_bucket for more information.

Author
John Hurliman jhurl.nosp@m.iman.nosp@m.@cull.nosp@m..tv
Parameters
{Number}bucketSize Maximum number of tokens to hold in the bucket. Also known as the burst rate.
{Number}tokensPerInterval Number of tokens to drip into the bucket over the course of one interval.
{String|Number}interval The interval length in milliseconds, or as one of the following strings: 'second', 'minute', 'hour', day'.
{TokenBucket}parentBucket Optional. A token bucket that will act as the parent of this bucket.
